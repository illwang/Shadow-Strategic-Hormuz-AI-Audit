### 2. Source Verification & Reproducibility Protocol

Every material judgment in this report is accompanied by an **independent verification path** that is:

- **Observable**: The primary source document is publicly accessible via official websites, regulatory portals, or archived media.
- **Quantifiable**: Key metrics are derived from countable, measurable data fields (e.g., regulatory filing entries, customs HS codes, exchange warehouse receipts).
- **Reproducible**: Any institutional analyst can replicate the core calculations using the step-by-step templates provided in Sections III and VII, without requiring special access privileges.

**Source Conflict Resolution**: When multiple sources present contradictory information, this report applies a hierarchical arbitration rule: Official regulatory announcements > Listed company disclosures > Industry statistics > Broker estimates > Media reports > Industry oral accounts. Peer-level conflicts are resolved in favor of the more recently published primary source.
